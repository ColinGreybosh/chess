Hello, and welcome to Chess! Here you and a friend could play fully-fledged matches of chess, the classic game of strategy and wit. Originating in the 6th century, chess continues to be one of the world's most popular games for both professionals and amatuers alike. So, how do you play?

In chess there are two sides, white and black, that play against each other. Each side has 16 total pieces consisting of the following: one king, one queen, two bishops, two rooks, and eight pawns. Each type of piece has it's own unique way of moving on the board. You can learn more about this on the Moves page! The object of the game is to put the opponent's king in a position of imminent capture, known as checkmate. There are numerous strategies discussed on this site that you could implement into your matches.

On the homepage you will find the game itself, where you can begin playing immediately. To restart the game at any time, simply click the restart button or refresh the page. Enjoy!
